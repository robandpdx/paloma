BUTTON STATES BASED ON SELECTION:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

No repositories selected:
  [Load CSV File] [Start selected]* [Reset selected]* [⚙️]* [Add Repository]
  * = disabled

One pending repo selected (java-private-library):
  [Load CSV File] [Start selected] [Reset selected]* [⚙️] [Add Repository]
  * = disabled (no repos to reset)

One in-progress repo selected (superbigmono):
  [Load CSV File] [Start selected]* [Reset selected] [⚙️]* [Add Repository]
  * = disabled (can't start/update settings for in-progress repos)

Multiple repos selected (superbigmono + my-react-app):
  [Load CSV File] [Start selected]* [Reset selected] [⚙️]* [Add Repository]
  * = disabled (none are pending/reset)
